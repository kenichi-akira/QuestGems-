CUBE
CUBE is a premium in-game currency in Axie Champions. CUBE is used to purchase and use items or features required for Axie Champions core gameplay.
 
AXS
Launched in November 2020, it is relatively new in the Axie Infiniti world. AXS is the ERC-20 governance token for the Axi Universe.
 
RONIN
Launched in January 2022, RON is the native token of the RONIN Blockchain. A key component of the Ecosystem developed by Sky Mavis for Axie Infinity.
